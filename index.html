The Campus Friction Audit — Assignment Survey

You are an independent consultant. FLAME University has hired you to audit one specific vertical of the campus experience. Your goal is to quantify friction with data, not to complain.

Choose one of the domains (each student selects a different domain):

  • The Commute: shuttle service schedule, comfort, connectivity (Aditi)
  • The Mess Hall: quality, hygiene, variety of buffet meals (Abhipsha)
  • The Campus Cafes: price, speed of service, variety (Diya)
  • The Vivekananda Library: study environment, book availability, noise (Navreet)
  • The Gym and Sports: equipment access, court maintenance, booking (Chhavi)
  • The Health Centre: doctor availability, response speed, pharmacy (Akshita)
  • Hostel Housekeeping: cleaning frequency, laundry turnaround, hygiene (Sanya)
  • Information Technology Services: Wi‑Fi speed, LMS access, tech support (Pranav Shinde)
  • The Program Office: transcripts, bonafide letters, ID cards (Dhruv Bishnoi)
  • Career Services: internships, placement guidance, resume workshops (Aryan)
  • Student Clubs and Events: event quality and joining ease (Pranav Vijay)

Survey instructions

  1. Design a 10-question survey specific to your assigned domain.
  2. Include a Net Promoter Score question (e.g., how likely to recommend).
  3. Include a Customer Effort Score (e.g., how easy was it to accomplish X).
  4. Include a Behavioral Frequency question (e.g., how many times this week).

Data handling: When you complete the survey your response is recorded on the server in all_responses.json. Responses are not displayed or downloadable from this landing page to protect privacy for the assignment.
